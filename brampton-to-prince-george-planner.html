The Road · 7 days · ~4,150 km

Trans-Canada / Yellowhead route through Ontario → Manitoba → Saskatchewan → Alberta → BC. Via Saskatoon (skipping Calgary) — shortest route. Arrives Prince George on July 31 to match your hotel reservation.

⚠ Jasper update (post-2024 wildfire): Town & Hwy 16 are fully open in 2026, but ~25% of hotel rooms are gone (some properties burned down). Book Jasper accommodation NOW — Tonquin Inn, Pyramid Lake Lodge & Marmot Lodge are confirmed operating; verify availability before Day 6.
Time-zone changes: −1 hr entering Manitoba (Day 3) · −1 hr entering Alberta (Day 5) · −1 hr entering BC (Day 7). Adjust Akshara's nap/meal times accordingly.
